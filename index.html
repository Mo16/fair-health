What are we

FairHealth - We are seeking to build an Insurance and healthcare aggregation platform - streamlining the global healthcare systems through the implementation of deep learning neural nets providing consumers with real-time recommendations and data-driven analytics to create a healthy global society.

Our Aim

We plan on creating a virtual hospital where our users can channel medical professionals from across the globe, as well as building a AI based tool (AISD) which delivers symptom analysis and diagnostics via this new technology. This will enable users to live a healthier lifestyle and to avoid illness and disease much more efficiently than is currently possible with todays methods. This includes an aggregate insurance system where our users can obtain and identify the best possible insurance plan based on their medical history.
